Explore Top eSIM Advantages for Your Journey

Instant & Easy Activation

On arrival in Canada you simply activate it — no need to hunt for a local store or wait for a physical SIM to arrive. This saves you the hassle at airports or in unfamiliar cities right after landing.

Avoid Expensive Roaming Charges

Using eSIM means you can connect to local Canadian carriers rather than relying on expensive international roaming. Many travel-eSIM plans offer data (and sometimes calls/SMS) at rates similar to those of domestic users.

Keep Home Number Active

With eSIM, you can often have more than one SIM profile on your device simultaneously (home and travel number). That means you don’t have to switch completely away from your country number.

Less Risk of SIM-Swap Fraud

Physical SIMs can be stolen or swapped by scammers. eSIM adds an extra layer of digital security that makes unauthorized switching harder.

No Waiting for Delivery

Unlike a physical SIM ordered online, you don’t need to wait days to receive it. An eSIM QR code arrives in few days.
